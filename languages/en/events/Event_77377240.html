Marriage

Marriage
Man Richard BARTLETT
Woman Linda "Lydia" QUESTER

Notes
Richard and Lydia arrived in Sydney on the "Lalla Rookh" on Boxing Day 1841 with daughter Sarah. By 1842 he was working in the Shoalhaven for David Berry. They moved to Tomerong in 1851, then on to Milton. They had 12 children and ran a butcher's shop near the Congregrational Church in Milton. Richard left the shop to Sarah Higgins, wife of his son William, who ran the shop until they moved to Nowra in the 1920's.
