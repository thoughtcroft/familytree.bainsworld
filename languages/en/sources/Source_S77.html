Text
In 1985, I collected and arranged an exhibition of photographs of early Milton-Ulladulla families for the Milton Settlers' Fair, held each year during the October long weekend. Over a few weeks, I had borrowed four hundred and fifty photographs from local desendants, everything from tiny snapshots to huge portraits in ornate frames, together with the families' histories. By this time, I couldn't bear to see this unique collection dispersed without being recorded, so with the owners' permission, the pictures were all copied by Mr Russell Smith , of Milton, before being returned.

Since then I have located and added another ninety five families to the original hundred and Russell has gone on patiently copying them. I am enormously grateful for his enthusiasm and expertise.
